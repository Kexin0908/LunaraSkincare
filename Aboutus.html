Brands We Carry

Skintific

Founded in recent years, Skintific is a science-based skincare brand from Asia. It focuses on barrier-repair technology and dermatologist-backed formulations, making it popular for sensitive and damaged skin solutions.

La Roche-Posay

Established in France in 1975, La Roche-Posay partners with dermatologists worldwide. Known for its thermal spring water, it specializes in sun care and treatments for sensitive skin.

COSRX

Founded in 2013 in South Korea, COSRX combines minimal ingredients with powerful results. The brand is especially known for its snail mucin and acne solutions, earning global recognition.

Eucerin

With roots tracing back to 1900 in Germany, Eucerin is one of the oldest dermatological brands. It offers clinically tested skincare, trusted for dry, sensitive, and aging skin worldwide.
